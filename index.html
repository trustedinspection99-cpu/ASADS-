Toronto Home Inspection FAQ

Answers to the most common questions from GTA home buyers and sellers.

How much does a home inspection cost in Toronto?

Toronto home inspection prices range from $400-$700 depending on property size, age, and type (house vs condo). Our pricing includes thermal imaging and same-day digital reports. Call 647-801-9311 for an exact quote based on your specific property.

What areas of Toronto do you serve for home inspections?

We serve all Greater Toronto Area communities including Toronto, Mississauga, Brampton, Vaughan, Markham, Oakville, Hamilton, Richmond Hill, Pickering, Ajax, Whitby, and surrounding areas within 75km of downtown Toronto.

How soon can you inspect a home in the GTA?

We typically schedule inspections within 24-48 hours in the GTA market. For urgent pre-purchase situations, we offer same-day service when availability permits. Call 647-801-9311 for immediate booking.

Do condo inspections differ from house inspections in Toronto?

Yes. Toronto condo inspections focus on interior systems (electrical, plumbing, HVAC within unit), windows, balconies, and visible structure. We also review status certificates - crucial for downtown Toronto condo purchases.

What's the most common issue found in GTA home inspections?

In the GTA, common issues include: roof wear (especially in older Toronto neighborhoods), foundation cracks (clay soil areas), outdated electrical (knob & tube in pre-1950 homes), and basement moisture issues.

Should I get a new construction inspection in the GTA?

Absolutely. Even new homes in Toronto suburbs have defects. A pre-closing inspection documents issues for builder correction under your Tarion warranty, potentially saving thousands.

Are you available for weekend inspections in Toronto?

Yes! We inspect 7 days a week to accommodate Toronto's fast real estate market. Weekend and evening appointments are available. Call 647-801-9311 to book.
