Why You Need a Professional 30-Day Inspection

In Ontario, the first 30 days of homeownership are critical. This is your primary window to submit a **30-Day Form to Tarion** documenting every surface-level and functional defect. Most homeowners lack the tools to find hidden leaks or structural shifts‚Äîthat's where ASADS comes in.

What Our GTA Inspectors Uncover:

  • Thermal Imaging Analysis: We find missing insulation in walls and moisture behind drywall that the naked eye misses.

  • Finishing Details: Systematic review of flooring, trim, cabinetry, and window seals.
