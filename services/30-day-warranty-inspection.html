Why You Need a Professional 30-Day Tarion Inspection

Most new homeowners lack the tools and expertise to identify hidden defects in their new construction. Missing your 30-day deadline means paying for repairs out of pocket. Our comprehensive inspection includes thermal imaging to detect issues invisible to the naked eye.

What Our Certified Inspectors Uncover:

  • Thermal Imaging Analysis: Detect missing insulation, moisture intrusion, and air leaks behind walls
  • Structural & Foundation: Check for settlement cracks, improper grading, and drainage issues
  • HVAC Systems: Verify furnace operation, HRV balancing, and ductwork connections
  • Electrical Safety: Test GFCI outlets, check panel installations, and verify code compliance
  • Building Envelope: Inspect windows, doors, siding, and roofing for proper installation
  • Interior Finishes: Document flooring, trim, painting, and cabinetry deficiencies
  • Plumbing Systems: Check for leaks, proper drainage, and fixture installation
  • Safety Compliance: Verify smoke/CO detectors meet Ontario Building Code requirements
