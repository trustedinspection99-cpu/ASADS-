Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a home inspection take in Ajax?

Most Ajax homes take 2-3 hours for a thorough inspection. Condos and townhouses typically take 1.5-2 hours. Larger or older homes may take longer.

Do you inspect the roof in Ajax?

Yes, we inspect all accessible roof areas from ground level and using ladders when safe. We examine shingles, flashing, gutters, and look for signs of leaks or damage common in Ajax's climate.

Can I attend the inspection?

Absolutely! We encourage clients to join us for the last 30-45 minutes of the inspection. We'll walk you through our findings and answer any questions about the Ajax property.
