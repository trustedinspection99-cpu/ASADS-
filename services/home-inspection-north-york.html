How do North York's ravine properties require different inspection approaches?

Answer: North York's ravine homes (York Mills, Don Valley) demand specialized assessment for: 1) Slope stability and erosion risks near watercourses and unstable soil conditions, 2) Foundation waterproofing effectiveness in high-moisture environments with seasonal water table fluctuations, 3) Tree root intrusion analysis into foundations and drainage systems from mature ravine trees, 4) Wildlife intrusion prevention and damage assessment from urban wildlife, 5) Specialty drainage requirements for sloped lots with unique water management needs, 6) Sewer line integrity evaluation in areas with soil movement and tree root penetration, 7) Deck and retaining wall safety assessments on uneven terrain, and 8) Environmental protection bylaw compliance for ravine lot alterations. We're experienced with Toronto's unique ravine properties.

What are common issues in North York's 1960s-1980s bungalows and split-level homes?

We specialize in mid-century home assessments.
