Home Systems Performance Testing & Energy Efficiency Audit

Comprehensive Home Systems Performance Evaluation

Our Home Systems Performance Testing in Toronto provides detailed evaluation of all major home systems to ensure optimal operation, efficiency, and reliability. We conduct specialized testing procedures that go beyond visual inspection to measure actual system performance, identify hidden inefficiencies, and detect early signs of impending failure. Our performance testing includes quantitative measurements, efficiency calculations, and comparative analysis against manufacturer specifications and industry standards.

For HVAC systems, we conduct combustion analysis, airflow measurements, refrigerant charge verification, heat exchange efficiency testing, and distribution system performance evaluation. Electrical system testing includes load analysis, voltage drop measurements, ground fault circuit interrupter testing, surge protection verification, and safety ground testing. Plumbing system evaluation includes water pressure and flow rate measurements, drainage flow testing, water heater efficiency analysis, and fixture performance assessment. Building envelope testing includes thermal imaging analysis, air leakage quantification, moisture intrusion detection, and insulation effectiveness evaluation.

Home Safety Inspection & Property Preservation Assessment

Comprehensive Home Safety System Evaluation

Our Home Safety Inspection in Toronto provides thorough evaluation of all safety systems and components to ensure your family's protection and compliance with current safety standards. We conduct detailed testing of fire safety systems, electrical safety features, structural safety elements, environmental safety concerns, and accessibility safety considerations. Our safety inspection identifies potential hazards, verifies system functionality, and provides specific recommendations for safety improvements and risk reduction.

Fire safety evaluation includes testing of smoke detectors, carbon monoxide detectors, fire extinguishers, emergency lighting, exit signs, fire separation integrity, and means of egress compliance. Electrical safety assessment includes verification of ground fault circuit interrupters, arc fault circuit interrupters, proper circuit identification, panel board safety, and wiring condition. Structural safety review includes evaluation of foundation stability, stairway safety, railing and guardrail integrity, deck and balcony safety, and load-bearing element condition. Environmental safety assessment includes testing for radon gas, carbon monoxide levels, mold presence, asbestos materials, lead paint, and indoor air quality concerns.

Safety Inspection Priority Classification

  • Missing or inoperative GFCI protection

  • Blocked emergency exits
