Professional Backyard Construction in Toronto

A great backyard starts with a solid foundation. In Toronto, the ground freezes and thaws constantly, which can cause cheap decks and fences to lean or crack. At ASADS, we use certified building science to ensure your project stays level and strong for decades.

Why Our Decks Are Stronger

4-Foot Concrete Footings

We dig every deck support 48 inches deep. This gets below the "frost line," so the freezing ground can't push your deck upward in the winter. This is crucial for Toronto's climate where we get 30+ freeze-thaw cycles each year.

Heavy-Duty Framing

We use thick, pressure-treated lumber for the skeleton of your deck. This wood is specially treated to resist rot, mold, and Toronto‚Äôs humidity. All our framing meets or exceeds Ontario Building Code requirements.

Proper Water Drainage

We slope deck boards slightly so rainwater runs off instead of pooling. This prevents rot and extends the life of your deck. We also ensure proper spacing between boards for drainage and expansion.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you handle Toronto building permits for decks?

Yes! In most GTA cities, any deck higher than 24 inches (2 feet) off the ground needs a building permit. We handle all the paperwork with your local building department.

How long does a typical deck installation take?

Most standard decks (300-400 sq.ft.) take 4-6 days from start to finish. This includes digging footings, building the frame, and installing the decking boards.

Can you build in the winter?

Yes, we work year-round. For winter installations, we use special techniques and materials to ensure proper concrete curing and construction quality.
