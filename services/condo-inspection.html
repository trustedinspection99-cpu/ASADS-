Expert Suites Inspections: Beyond the Surface

A condo inspection is different from a house inspection. We focus on the high-risk components of high-rise living that often lead to "Special Assessments" or expensive suite repairs.

What We Inspect in Your Suite:

  • Fan Coil Units (HVAC): Testing actuators, filter health, and condensate drain pans.
  • Thermal Imaging: Scanning for moisture from units above and energy loss in window walls.
  • Plumbing Safety: Identifying Kitec piping and testing shut-off valves.
  • Electrical Panel: Full safety check of the suite breaker panel and GFCIs.
  • Appliances: Functional testing of all in-suite kitchen and laundry machines.

Answering Real Condo Buyer Questions

Does the inspector check the building's common elements?

We focus on the interior suite. However, we look for visible clues on balconies or windows that suggest systemic building envelope issues.

What is the most common issue in Toronto condos?

Faulty Fan Coil Unit (HVAC) actuators and slow leaks behind laundry setups are our most frequent findings in the GTA.
