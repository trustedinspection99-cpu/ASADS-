Expert Suite Inspections: Beyond the Surface

A condo inspection is significantly different from a house inspection. While the building envelope is usually a common element, the interior components are your responsibility. We focus on high-risk areas that lead to costly suite-owner repairs.

What We Inspect in Your Suite:

  • Fan Coil Units (HVAC): Testing actuators, filter health, and checking for mold in condensate drain pans.
  • Thermal Imaging: Using infrared technology to scan for moisture intrusion from the units above and air leaks in window walls.
  • Plumbing Safety: Identifying Kitec piping and testing main shut-off valves for functionality.
  • Electrical Panel: Full safety audit of the suite‚Äôs breaker panel, GFCIs, and potential aluminum wiring in older buildings.
  • Laundry & Appliances: Functional testing of in-suite machines, specifically focusing on dryer venting and washer hose integrity.

Answering Real Condo Buyer Questions

Does the inspector check the building's common elements?

We focus on the interior suite. However, we check the balcony, windows, and exterior doors for signs of systemic building envelope issues that could trigger future special assessments.

What is the most common issue in Toronto condos?

Faulty Fan Coil Unit (HVAC) actuators and moisture hidden behind laundry drywall are the most frequent findings in the GTA market.
