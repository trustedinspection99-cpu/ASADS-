Mississauga Home Inspection FAQ

How much does a home inspection cost in Mississauga?

Our pricing is based on square footage, age, and type of property. Typical range is $450-$750 for a standard single-family home. Condo inspections start at $350, and luxury homes may be higher. We provide free quotes with no obligation.

Do you inspect high-rise condos in Square One area?

Yes! We specialize in condominium inspections including high-rise buildings. We inspect the unit interior, balcony, parking space, and review condo documents for important information about common elements and reserve funds.

What special concerns should I know about in Port Credit homes?

Port Credit's lakefront location and heritage properties require special attention to flood risk, foundation moisture, and older construction methods. We also check for proper flood-proofing in lower levels and evaluate sump pump systems.

How soon can I book an inspection in Mississauga?
