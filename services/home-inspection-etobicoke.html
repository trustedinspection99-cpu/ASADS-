Etobicoke Home Inspection Questions

How much does a home inspection cost in Etobicoke?

Etobicoke home inspection prices typically range from $450 to $750 depending on property size, age, and additional services like thermal imaging or asbestos testing. Heritage homes in The Kingsway or waterfront properties may be at the higher end. We provide free quotes with no obligation.

Do you inspect homes with aluminum wiring in Etobicoke?

Yes, we frequently inspect homes with aluminum wiring common in 1960s-1970s Etobicoke neighborhoods. We check for proper connections, oxidation, and safety issues, and provide recommendations for remediation if needed.

How long does a home inspection take in Etobicoke?

Most Etobicoke home inspections take 2-3 hours for a typical single-family home. Larger heritage homes in The Kingsway or properties with multiple additions may take longer. We never rush inspections to ensure thorough evaluation.

What's included in your Etobicoke home inspection?

Our comprehensive inspection covers structure, roof, exterior, interior, electrical, plumbing, heating, cooling, insulation, ventilation, and built-in appliances. Thermal imaging is included at no extra cost to detect hidden moisture and insulation issues.

Can I attend the inspection?

Yes, we encourage clients to attend the final hour of the inspection for a walkthrough where we explain our findings and answer questions about the property. This is especially valuable for first-time home buyers in Etobicoke.
