Modern homes demand more power than ever before. Between high-efficiency HVAC systems, multiple workstations, and electric vehicles, old infrastructure often fails to keep up. At ASADS, we bridge the gap between legacy wiring and modern safety requirements.

Critical Fire Safety Note

Homes with Federal Pacific (Stab-Lok) breakers or Aluminum wiring are often flagged by insurance providers. These components are prone to overheating and failure. We provide certified remediation and safety letters.
