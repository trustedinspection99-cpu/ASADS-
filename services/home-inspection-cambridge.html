Cambridge Home Inspection Questions

How much does a home inspection cost in Cambridge?

Cambridge home inspection prices typically range from $400 to $700 depending on property size, age, and additional services like thermal imaging or radon testing. We provide free quotes with no obligation.

How long does a home inspection take in Cambridge?

Most Cambridge home inspections take 2-3 hours for a typical single-family home. Larger or older properties may take longer. We never rush inspections to ensure thorough evaluation.

Do you inspect century homes in Cambridge?

Yes, we specialize in heritage home inspections in Cambridge's historic districts. We're experienced with older construction methods, materials, and common issues in century homes.

What's included in your Cambridge home inspection?

Our comprehensive inspection covers structure, roof, exterior, interior, electrical, plumbing, heating, cooling, insulation, ventilation, and built-in appliances. Thermal imaging is included at no extra cost.

Can I attend the inspection?

Yes, we encourage clients to attend the final hour of the inspection for a walkthrough where we explain our findings and answer questions about the property.
