Specializing in Legal Second Units

Brampton Secondary Suite Compliance

Thinking of buying a home with a rental suite? We verify the critical safety elements that the City of Brampton requires for legal registration:

  • Fire Separation: Verifying 30-minute or 1-hour fire ratings between units, proper drywall installation
  • Egress Windows: Ensuring basement bedroom windows meet minimum opening size requirements (at least 15" high, 24" wide)
  • HVAC & Ventilation: Checking for proper ducting, heating, and ventilation between units
  • Smoke & CO Alarms: Verifying interconnected alarms on every level and outside sleeping areas
  • Separate Utilities: Assessing electrical panels, meters, and utility access
  • Parking & Exits: Evaluating parking spaces and safe exit pathways

Brampton Home Inspection FAQ

Do you check for legal basement apartment (Second Unit) compliance in Brampton?

Yes. We specifically inspect for fire separation (drywall thickness), window egress sizes, and independent HVAC ducting requirements as per Brampton's Second Unit registration standards. Our report will indicate which elements meet current requirements.

How much does a home inspection cost in Brampton?

Brampton inspections typically range from $450 to $700. We offer competitive rates for large estate homes in Castlemore and townhomes in Mount Pleasant. Condo inspections start at $350. Contact us for a free quote based on your specific property.

Is a home inspection worth it for a brand new house in Brampton?

Absolutely. Many new builds in Mount Pleasant and East Brampton have missing insulation, reversed wiring, or poor grading. Our Tarion PDI inspections protect your warranty rights and ensure you're getting the quality you paid for.

Do you provide commercial inspections in Brampton?

Yes. We offer inspections for small commercial units, plazas, and multi-unit residential buildings across the Peel Region. We can assess retail spaces, office units, and mixed-use properties.
