The Deep-Dive Technical Checklist

Our 500-point inspection protocol covers every square inch of the property. We provide an exhaustive analysis of the following critical clusters:

1. Roofing & Building Envelope

We evaluate asphalt shingles for granular loss, cupping, and clawing. We check for proper **ice damming** protection (Ice & Water Shield) and ensure the attic ventilation meets the 1/300 ratio required by the Ontario Building Code.

2. Structural & Foundation

Analysis of poured concrete, cinder block, and stone foundations. We identify **settlement cracks**, step-cracks in brick veneer, and sagged floor joists. We check for "Post-and-Beam" integrity and sill plate rot.

3. Electrical Safety Standards

Exposing outdated **Knob-and-Tube wiring**, ungrounded branch circuits, and dangerous **Aluminum wiring** connections. We inspect electrical panels for Federal Pacific or Zinsco components known to be fire hazards.

4. Plumbing & Waste Management

Identifying **Kitec plumbing**, galvanized supply lines, and polybutylene piping. We check for **cross-connections**, proper P-traps, and verify the operation of sump pumps and backwater valves.

5. HVAC & Interior Climate

Testing gas-fired furnaces for cracked heat exchangers (Carbon Monoxide risk) and evaluating AC compressor health. We also inspect HRV/ERV systems for proper air exchange rates.

6. Environmental Hazards

Visual identification of potential **Asbestos-Containing Materials (ACM)** like vermiculite or 9x9 floor tiles. We also scan for visible mold and provide air quality testing if required.

Understanding Your Inspection Report

Your ASADS report is a legal-grade document delivered the same day. Each deficiency is categorized by severity:

  • Major Defect: Structural failures or systems that have reached the end of their useful life (e.g., a 20-year-old roof or failed furnace).
  • Safety Hazard: Immediate risks such as missing GFCI protection, exposed wiring, or gas leaks.
  • Repair/Monitor: Minor issues like weathered caulking or slow faucets that can be addressed post-closing.
